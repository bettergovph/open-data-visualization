About Us

Learn more about our mission and team

Our Mission

BetterGovPH is dedicated to promoting transparency, accountability, and accessibility in Philippine government operations. We believe that open data is essential for informed citizen participation, effective governance, and sustainable development.

Through our data visualization platform, we make complex government information accessible to everyone - from citizens and journalists to policymakers and researchers. Our tools transform raw government data into meaningful insights that drive informed decision-making and public discourse.

Our Vision

To create a Philippines where government data is openly accessible, easily understandable, and actively used by citizens to hold their government accountable and participate in the democratic process.

Our Approach

We focus on three core principles that guide everything we do:

  • Open Data Advocacy: Championing government transparency and public access to information
  • Data Accessibility: Transforming complex datasets into user-friendly visualizations and insights
  • Citizen Empowerment: Enabling informed participation in governance and policy discussions
  • Technical Excellence: Building robust, reliable tools using modern web technologies
  • Collaborative Development: Working with government agencies, civil society, and citizens

Our Principles

What Guides Us

Open Access

We believe government data belongs to the people. We work tirelessly to ensure public information is freely accessible and usable by everyone.

Citizen Empowerment

We empower Filipinos with tools and knowledge to actively participate in governance, hold leaders accountable, and shape their communities.

Transparency

We advocate for government transparency as the foundation of democracy, ensuring that public spending and infrastructure decisions are open to public scrutiny.

Open Source

All our tools and code are open source, enabling collaboration, peer review, and community-driven improvements to serve the public interest.
